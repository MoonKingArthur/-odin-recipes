Egg Drop Soup (15 Minutes)

Intro:

Let me preface by saying that I love egg drop soup. It's delicious, and nobody can tell me otherwise.

Prep time: 5 mins

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Eggs (lightly beaten)
  • Green onions chopped (optional)
  • Sriracha sauce

Instructions:

  1. Bring the chicken stock to a simmer in a medium soup pot.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and sriracha to taste.
  3. Use a ladle to stir the soup in a circular motion, and slowly drizzle in the egg until you have added it all.
  4. Ladle the soup into bowls, top with green onions, and serve!
