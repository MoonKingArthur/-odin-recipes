Boiled Potatoes (35 minutes)

Intro:

If you're not hungry enough to eat a boiled potato, you're not hungry

Prep time: 5 mins

Ingredients:

  • Russet Potatoes

Instructions:

  1. Place potatoes in a medium soup pot and bring it to a simmer.
  2. Let it boil for 25-30 minutes
  3. Use a fork to check doneness, then scoop onto a plate and enjoy!
