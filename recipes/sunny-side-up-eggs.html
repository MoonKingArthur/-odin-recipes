Sunny Side up egg (15 Minutes)

Intro:

Yep, we're only making one sunny side up egg for this recipe

Prep time: 5 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a medium nonstick skillet over low heat until slightly shimmering
  2. Crack an egg into the skillet/li>
  3. Cover with a tight lid and cook, uninterrupted, until the whites are completely set but the yolks are still runny
  4. Slide the eggs out of the skillet onto a plate
  5. Season with salt and pepper then enjoy!
